Welcome to Rocket Patrol: Modded Edition!

Controls:
  During 1P Game:
    Move left with ←
    Move right with →
    Fire with (F)
  During 2P Game:
    Player 1 (Red):
      Move left with (A)
      Move right with (D)
      Fire with (W)
    Player 2 (Blue):
      Move left with ←
      Move right with →
      Fire with ↑
